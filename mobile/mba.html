Master of Business Administration

Objectives:

  1. to provide a knowledge base of interdisplinary business theories and techniques to students

  2. to train and to develop students' practical skills in a chosen concentrated area for career development

  3. to develop the students' decision-making and leadership capabilities to face the challenges of a dynamic business world staged with diverse, multicultural and global business settings

Background Preparation:

  • Preparatory Module A(PBUS01): Essentials of Management and Business Law

  • Preparatory Module B(PBUS02): Essentials of Economicals and Marketing

  • Preparatory Module C(PBUS03): Essentials of Accounting and Finance

  • Preparatory Module D(PBUS04): Essentials of Quantitatives Analysis and Information Technology

PBUS03 covers accounting and finance subjects at basic level(ACC201 and FIN310). Students choosing the accounting concentration must also have the background in ACC201L, ACC202 and ACC202L.

Graduation Requirements:

A minimum of 36 units are required for graduation.

  • 15 units of foundation courses

  • 12-15 units of major courses

  • 3-6 units of electives

  • 3 units of capstone
