Master of Science in Computer Science

Objectives:

The MSCS degree program intends to provide advanced knowledge and hands-on experience in computer science to the students who are interested in gaining expertise in software engineering as well as modern internet technologies and applications. Through the learning process, the students not only acquire knowledge in modern computer technologies but also cultivate the ability in the software design, development, deployment and integration aspects of professional learing. Specific studies of the program cover database technology, software engineering, internet technology, mobile computing, Unix/Linux programming and administration, and network engineering and security related fields.

Background Preparation:

  • Engineering Mathematics: MATH208

  • Computing Engineering Subject: CE305

  • Programming LanguageS and Data Structure: CS204 & Lab, CS350 & Lab, CS360 & Lab

  • Operating Systems: CS230 & Lab, CS380

A student with any deficiency is required to clear it by either taking the course at NPU and earning a grade of at least C or higher, or taking and passing a proficiency exam on the subject.

Graduation Requirements:

A minimum of 36 units are required for graduation.

  • 12 units of foundation courses

  • 12 units of major courses

  • 9 units of electives

  • 3 units of capstone
